Campus Life

delhi public school-campus

DPS Allahabad can proudly boast of an enviable infrastructure. The school nestles in the lap of nature on the banks of the Ganga. The school is spread on 20 acres of verdant land. It has two courts for Basketball, three Deco Turf Tennis courts, an equestrian area with three American horses, a Splash Pool for the Tiny Tots and a playground for the students. The Ashish Nehra Cricket academy is also being opened shortly. It also has an open air stage and an auditorium. There are well equipped labs for Science, Computer Science, Psychology, Home Science, Languages and Maths. The classes are digitally enabled. Medical facilities, RO water and a canteen serving nutritious food are also available to students and staff.

To kindle a conductive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide,Delh Public School has spared no effort or investement to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them pursue their education with ease and comfort.
